David Åhman

Klas David Åhman (born 20 December 2001) is a Swedish professional beach volleyball player. Together with Jonatan Hellvig, he won the gold medal at the 2024 Summer Olympics and the silver medal at the 2023 Beach Volleyball World Championships. He is currently ranked number one on the FIVB Beach Volleyball World Ranking.
